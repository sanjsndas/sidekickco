Room‑by‑room setup

Living room: We place scratching posts near furniture (to save your sofa!), add washable rugs for traction, and create a “pet pathway” that keeps toys accessible yet tidy. Kitchen: Elevated feeders, non‑slip mats, and a dedicated washing station for bowls. Bedroom: A calming corner with a crate or bed, away from drafts but with a view of the family.

Our setup includes safety audits: we check cords, toxic plants, small objects, and unstable furniture. We also provide a customized “pet first‑aid” box with essentials like gauze, pet‑safe antiseptic, and a digital thermometer.

  • Puppy proofing (cables, corners, cabinets)
  • Kitten exploration zones (vertical & horizontal)
  • Senior pet accessibility (ramps, low‑entry beds)

Setup timeline & packages

We offer Express Setup (4 hours) for essentials, Full Day (8 hours) with deep organizing, and Weekend Retreat (2 days) that includes training sessions and enrichment planning. Each package starts with a virtual home walkthrough, followed by an on‑site visit.

Our team brings all supplies: beds, feeders, toys, litter boxes, grooming tools, and even calming pheromone diffusers. We also install smart devices like pet cameras and automatic feeders, and connect them to your phone.

  1. Day 1: Assessment & furniture rearrangement
  2. Day 2: Delivery & installation of equipment
  3. Day 3: Final walk‑through & behavior tips

We also provide a 30‑day follow‑up via video call to adjust any setups as your pet grows.

Enrichment & beyond

A well‑set home is only the beginning. We curate rotating toy stations to prevent boredom, design scent trails for mental stimulation, and suggest puzzle feeders that match your pet’s skill level. Our team also shares a digital “pet journal” with daily activity logs, feeding schedules, and milestone trackers.

For multi‑pet households, we create harmony zones — separate eating and resting areas to reduce rivalry, plus shared play spaces that encourage bonding. We’ve helped over 2,000 families integrate new pets smoothly, reducing anxiety and destructive behaviors.

Why proper setup matters: A study by the American Veterinary Society found that pets with structured environments show 40% fewer stress‑related issues. Sidekick Co’s approach reduces adjustment time from weeks to just days. Our clients report better sleep, fewer accidents, and a stronger bond with their pet from day one.
