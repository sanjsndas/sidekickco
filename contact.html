Why Contact Sidekick Co?

Whether you're bringing home a new puppy, kitten, or rescue pet, our team is dedicated to making the transition smooth and joyful. We understand that every home and every pet is unique, which is why we offer personalized setup consultations tailored to your space and lifestyle.

Our experts are available to answer questions about:

  • Pet‑proofing your home (cords, plants, furniture)
  • Choosing the right bed, crate, or carrier
  • Feeding stations, litter boxes, and grooming areas
  • Enrichment activities and toy rotation
  • Multi‑pet harmony and space planning

We also offer virtual consultations for clients outside our immediate service area. Just let us know your needs, and we'll find a solution that works for you and your pet.

What to Expect When You Reach Out

When you contact Sidekick Co, you can expect a friendly, professional response within 24 hours (or sooner during business hours). Our process typically involves:

  1. Initial conversation: We'll listen to your goals, concerns, and your pet's personality.
  2. Home assessment: We'll schedule an in‑person or virtual walkthrough of your space.
  3. Customized plan: We'll propose a setup package that fits your budget and timeline.
  4. Installation & training: Our team will set up equipment and show you how to maintain it.
  5. Follow‑up: We check in after 30 days to ensure everything is working perfectly.
