Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. Learn how we use them and your choices.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you don’t have to re‑enter them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the site owners. They are not harmful — they do not contain viruses or malware, and they cannot access your hard drive or personal files.

Did you know? The first cookie was created in 1994 by Lou Montulli, a Netscape engineer, to help websites remember users' shopping carts.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following specific purposes:

  • Authentication & Security: To verify your identity and protect against fraudulent activity.
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and improve your browsing experience.
  • Analytics: To understand how you interact with our site, which pages you visit, and how long you stay. This helps us optimize content and design.
  • Marketing: To tailor promotional messages and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns (only with your explicit consent).
  • Third‑Party Integrations: To enable features like embedded videos, maps, and social sharing buttons.

We do not use cookies to track you across other websites or to build detailed profiles without your consent.

4. Duration of Cookies

Cookies can be either session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (remain on your device for a set period). We use both types:

  • Session cookies: Used for temporary operations like navigating between pages or filling forms. They expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: Used for remembering preferences and analytics. Their lifespan varies from a few days to up to 2 years, after which they are automatically deleted.

You can manually delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. However, deleting essential cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Your Choices & Consent

When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to Accept All, Decline, or Customize Settings (choose which categories you allow). You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the “Cookie Settings” link in the footer or using the consent dialogue at the bottom of this page.

Your rights: You have the right to withdraw consent at any time. Refusing cookies may affect the availability of some features, but you can still use our core services.

Most browsers also allow you to manage cookies via their settings. You can block all cookies, delete existing ones, or set alerts before a cookie is stored. Please note that blocking all cookies may break certain website functionalities.

7. Cookie Categories in Detail

7.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core features such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies as they are required for the service.

  • Cookie: session_id — Used to maintain your session and authenticate you.
  • Cookie: csrf_token — Protects against cross‑site request forgery.

7.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our site, such as which pages are most popular and how users navigate. We use this data to improve our site’s performance and user experience. All data is aggregated and anonymized.

  • Cookie: _ga (Google Analytics) — Distinguishes unique users.
  • Cookie: _gid (Google Analytics) — Used to throttle request rates.

7.3 Functionality Cookies

These allow our site to remember choices you make (e.g., language, region) and provide enhanced, personalized features. They may be set by us or by third‑party providers whose services we use.

  • Cookie: language_pref — Stores your preferred language.
  • Cookie: region — Stores your region for localized content.

7.4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. They are placed by us or by our advertising partners. We only use these with your explicit consent.

  • Cookie: _fbp (Facebook Pixel) — Enables targeted ads.
  • Cookie: _gcl_au (Google Ads) — Conversion tracking.
